⚡ How The Bot Works
PIPELINE
1
Fetch Live Market Data
Reads LTP, bid/ask prices, bid/ask quantities and volume from broker API every 100ms via WebSocket
2
Feature Engineering
Converts raw tick data into 6 AI features: MDR (buyer/seller ratio), volume delta, spread, trend, aggression, trade speed
3
ML Prediction
Random Forest (100 trees) classifies the next move as BUY_FAST (+₹1), SELL_FAST (-₹1), or NO_MOVE within 5 seconds
4
Trade Execution
Places BUY or SELL order at current LTP with ₹1 target and ₹1 stoploss. Skips when prediction is NO_MOVE
5
Exit Management
Monitors price every tick — exits on target hit 🎯, stoploss hit 🛑, or 5-second time limit ⏱️
6
Report Generation
Auto-generates trade_log.csv and a professional PDF report after session ends with full P&L breakdown
